  • TITLE : Nandagokula Santhe

    DESCRIPTION : The maiden funfair "Nandagokula Santhe "was a grand success. Our chairman ,Dr Rukmangada Naidu and Vice Chairman Mrs Jalajakshamma along with a few grandparents declared it open. We are really overwhelmed at the response and profusely thank our patrons for the wonderful support. It was a colourful sight, to see the quadrangle filled with small canopy shops full of varied things like sarees ,ornaments ,craft items, game stalls icecream stalls ,selfie booth and the mouth watering delicacies prepared by the teachers which got sold like hotcakes. The venue was heavily flooded with the people from all walks of life .The grandparents and parents thoroughly enjoyed the santhe and said that they would look forward for more. The children had a great time thronging to their teachers stall and buying something to eat with great pride.
